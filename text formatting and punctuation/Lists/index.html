    Lists

    Rule

    The serial or Oxford comma is mandatory in a list of items.

    When you are listing items, the final comma before the conjunction is optional. However, on Brilliant it is not. This comma announces the last item of the series visually, so we include it to ease scanning of the lesson text.

    No

    An ellipse is drawn using two tacks, a loop of string and a pencil.

    Yes

    An ellipse is drawn using two tacks, a loop of string, and a pencil.
