# Italic

!!!tldr "Rule"
    The only permitted use of italics is to denote the titles of books, movies, or other published materials.

Italicized terms are not easy to distinguish from normal text — they "hide out" until you arrive at them. The only use of italics prescribed for Brilliant lessons is to designate titles of books, movies, or other published materials.

You can convey conversational importance on a word by italicizing it, but understand that it is likely to be missed on a skim. If it is crucial that people notice your emphasis, always use bold.

!!! failure "Example"
    "Jack seemed happy, he *really* liked that birdwatching shirt you got him!"


!!! failure "Example"
    Which algorithm would *not* finish in polynomial time?


!!! success "Example"
    Which algorithm would **not** **finish in polynomial time?
