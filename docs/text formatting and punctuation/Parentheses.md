# Parentheses

!!!tldr "Rule"
    Parentheses can be used when an em dash won't suffice.

Parentheses are overloaded symbols in mathematical writing. Although their meaning is usually clear from context, they are primarily used in Brilliant lessons as mathematical symbols.

!!! success "Example"
    One particular parametric representation of the unit circle is $(\cos t, \sin t).$

In text, it is recommended to replace parentheses with em dashes.
